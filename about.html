Hi, I’m lakshika maduvanthi. I’m a 34-year-old self-taught artist who’s into a mix of traditional and digital art. Since 2018, I have been dedicated to honing my artistic skills, and I remain committed to delivering my best in every piece I create. My favorite mediums are graphite and color pencils for traditional portraits, and I also enjoy creating art digitally. Each piece is painstakingly crafted freehand, with a focus on creativity and detail.
